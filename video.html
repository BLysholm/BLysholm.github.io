Video Experience

I refined my video experience through my involvement in some prerecorded video and livestream work at Westgate Alliance Church. During my internship there, I recorded and edited their online video content. I was also heavily involved in setting up their video streaming setup and was one of the primary video switchers for over a year. This involved controlling PTZ cameras, which helped me refine my skills in framing shots.

For the past few years at Circle Drive Alliance, I have been overseeing but not directly handling the video components of our livestream, since I had a competent video volunteer team. I occasionally operated a camera, but it was not my primary focus. As a result, I do not have substantial video experience to display in my portfolio. However, I was involved in maintaining the video equipment, including training camera operators to troubleshoot their equipment for wear and tear.

I have experience with the following camera gear:

  • Blackmagic Ursa Mini G2 Cinema Camera
  • Blackmagic Pocket Cinema 6K Pro
  • Cannon DSLRs
  • Nikon DSLRs
