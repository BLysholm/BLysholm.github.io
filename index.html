This included finishing my Bachelor of Theology degree at Ambrose and completing an IT certificate program from Saskatchewan Polytechnic while working part time at Circle Drive Alliance Church as Technical Production Manager. Now I am looking for a church where I can serve in fulltime ministry leading their tech team.
