Audio Experience

My audo experience includes the following:

  • Live audio with digital and analog mixers
  • Mixing Livestream audio using Reaper as a DAW
  • Training new techs
Audio was the initial thing that drew me into the tech booth. I have a deep passion for mixing audio for worship, and equipping others to mix as well. I understand deeply that my decisions from the booth are not purely technical, but also communicate the church's theological values to the congregation.
At Circle Drive Alliance Church, one of my biggest successes was in assessing the resources available, and how to properly utilize them to increase our audio quality. This involved researching and implementing already owned gear in new ways, providing better support to our audio techs, and creating a culture that shared knowledge between onsite and online audio techs.
